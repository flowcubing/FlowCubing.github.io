2020 Cubing Timeline

This project started as an idea in March 2020, and is now finally realized. Inspired by the 2b2t timeline by Sato86, I wanted to create a timeline about speedcubing. So, after many weeks, I have created the 2020 Speedcubing Timeline. It features most of the major events that influenced speedcubing. To make this timeline, I used a lot of different sources. I have a bibliography at the bottom of the page. This may not be the most accurate, but it was fun to make. Let me know what you think.
